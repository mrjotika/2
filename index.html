Hello, My name's Martin. I've been creating digital products, user experiences and branding for over 20 years. I like things that are simple, creative and nonsense-free.

From bases in London and Melbourne I work remotely with start-ups and famous names from all over the world.

I've gained an unusually versatile skill set from a love of side projects, varied client work and six years with a SaaS start-up that was acquired in 2020.
